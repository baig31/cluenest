# ClueNest Engineering Principles

## Principle 1

Single Source of Truth.

Every Product Profile exists only once.

Everything references the Product Profile.

---

## Principle 2

Never duplicate data.

Always reference existing information.

---

## Principle 3

Business Rules before Code.

If the business rule is unclear, do not write code.

---

## Principle 4

Small commits.

Every Git commit should represent one logical change.

---

## Principle 5

Documentation First.

Complex features must be documented before implementation.

---

## Principle 6

WordPress is the Platform.

ClueNest is the Product.

---

## Principle 7

Simple First.

Build the MVP.

Improve later.

Avoid over-engineering.

---

## Principle 8

Every feature should save the administrator time.

---

## Principle 9

Performance matters from day one.

---

## Principle 10

Every module should be independently maintainable.
