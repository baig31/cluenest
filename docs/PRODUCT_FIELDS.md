# Product Profile Fields

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

A Product Profile is the Single Source of Truth for every product in ClueNest.

Buying Guides, Comparisons, Category Pages, and Brand Pages reference Product Profiles.

Product Profiles never duplicate data.
